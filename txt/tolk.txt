Patients that do not speak Dutch or have problems with the language may request an interpreter. The interpreter will ensure that you and the nurse or doctor will understand each other better. You can make the request at the department in the hospital where you have an appointment or where you have been admitted. Please state clearly in which language you would like to communicate in, so that the right interpreter is requested at the Interpreter and Translation Centre the Netherlands. This service is free of charge.If you have asylum seeker status with a registration card (RZA), please clearly state this with your request. This involves a different procedure for requesting an interpreter at the hospital. You may also arrange for an interpreter yourself by contacting the Dutch Council for Refugees.
